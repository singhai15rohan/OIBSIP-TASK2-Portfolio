About Me

Hello, I am Rohan Jain, a second-year student pursuing my B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). I have a strong foundation in Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) and a passion for Java and Web development, which I've honed through hands-on projects and practical experience.

Currently, I serve as a Technical Executive at the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) at Medi-Caps University, where I lead and manage technical projects. My proficiency in Java and DSA is evident in projects like a Number guessing game, ATM interface, and a Tic Tac Toe game, all developed in Java. Additionally, I've worked on a Web development project creating an Amazon clone, showcasing my skills in both frontend and backend development.

I pride myself on my adaptability and strong understanding of technical concepts, enabling me to effectively tackle complex problems. With a keen interest in learning and a knack for innovation, I am eager to contribute to the field of Computer Science and Engineering.
